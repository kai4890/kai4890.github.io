Prototipe (2018-2020)

Prototipe is a Multiplayer First Person Shooter set in a Sci-Fi Era, I worked on the game from late 2018 around 2 months after I finished the Ball Roller project to the end of 2020. To develop the game I enlisted the assistance from one of my classmates, Rhys Blaxland. Whereas I focused on all aspects of the game, Rhys lent a bit of assistance when it came to the coding aspect of the game. It was my first large scale game I worked on, despite my lack of experience I deliberately aimed high in order to challenge myself and learn new skills in the process.

Ball Roller X (2018)

My first venture in the world of Game Development: Ball Roller X, this project was what sparked my love for programming and set me down my current path. Based off a Unity tutorial I modified it to have 10 levels with increasing difficulty and a menu and level select system. It had 2 enemy types and collectibles the player had to collect in order to progress to the next level. Should the player fall off the edge or touch the enemies the player would automatically respawn. Despite the game being very barebones, with no audio or title screen, it's a project that I learnt a lot from and as a result grew as a programmer.
